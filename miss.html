Open when you miss me 🫂

Hey love, If you’re reading this right now, it probably means you’re missing me. And honestly… I’m probably missing you too every moment. I know distance isn’t always easy. There are days when we wish we could just sit together, talk for hours, or do nothing but still feel close. But even though we are far away, please remember that you are always in my thoughts. In the small moments of my day, in random songs, in little things that remind me of you. You are a part of my everyday life, even from miles away. So if you miss me, just imagine I’m sitting next to you right now, talking to you, laughing with you, and annoying you a little. And remember this — distance can’t change how much I care about you. We’ll meet again, and until then, a piece of my heart is always with you. — The girl who misses you too 💗
